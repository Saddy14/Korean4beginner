Grammar Lessons

Grammar 1: V - 아요 / 어요

Attached to verb or adjective stems to make sentences polite.

  • Stems with ㅏ, ㅗ → + 아요
  • 하다 → 해요
  • Stems with ㅓ, ㅜ, ㅣ → + 어요
공부하다 → 공부해요
자다 → 자요
먹다 → 먹어요

Grammar 2: N - 을 / 를

These are object particles.

  • 을 – used when the noun ends with a consonant
    책을 읽어요 → “(I) read a book”
  • 를 – used when the noun ends with a vowel
    영화를 봐요 → “(I) watch a movie.”

Grammar 3: N - 에서

Use when something happens at a place or starts from a place.

  • Place of Action → “at / in”
    학교에서 공부해요 → “I study at school.”
  • Starting Point (origin) → “from”
    한국에서 왔어요 → “I came from Korea.”
